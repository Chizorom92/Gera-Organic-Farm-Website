Our Poultry Farm

At GERA ORGANIC FARM , we are committed to providing high-quality, organically raised poultry and fish. Our farm combines natural farming methods with sustainable practices to deliver fresh, healthy, and eco-friendly products to our customers. Whether you're a wholesaler, retailer, or individual buyer, we have tailored solutions to meet your needs.

We specialize in raising healthy and organic chickens under the best welfare standards. Our birds are naturally fed, antibiotic-free, and grown in a stress-free environment to ensure top-quality meat and eggs.



What We Offer:

  • Broiler production (for meat supply)

  • Layer production (for egg supply)

  • Organic and free-range chicken rearing

  • Custom supply for hotels, restaurants, and distributors
